# Data Sources

This document outlines the official sources used to compile, validate, and curate the **African Countries: A Curated Dataset on Africa Indicators for Education and Data Science**.

## 1. Primary Sources

Indicators for all 54 African countries were researched and cross-validated against the following official and recognized sources:

- **World Bank Group – Surface area (sq. km)**
  - **Description:** Official World Bank indicator providing total land surface area in square kilometers for countries worldwide, updated to 2023.
  - **URL:** [https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/AG.SRF.TOTL.K2](https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/AG.SRF.TOTL.K2)

- **World Bank Group – Population, total**
  - **Description:** Official World Bank indicator providing total population estimates for countries worldwide, updated to 2024.
  - **URL:** [https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.POP.TOTL](https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.POP.TOTL)

- **World Atlas – Landlocked Countries in Africa**
  - **Description:** Reference article identifying and listing all landlocked nations on the African continent.
  - **URL:** [https://www.worldatlas.com/articles/landlocked-countries-in-africa.html](https://www.worldatlas.com/articles/landlocked-countries-in-africa.html)

- **Google Developers – Latitude and Longitude**
  - **Description:** Canonical dataset providing geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) for countries worldwide.
  - **URL:** [https://developers.google.com/public-data/docs/canonical/countries_csv](https://developers.google.com/public-data/docs/canonical/countries_csv)

- **Countries of the World – List of African Capitals**
  - **Description:** Reference list of capital cities for all African countries.
  - **URL:** [https://www.countries-ofthe-world.com/capitals-of-africa.html](https://www.countries-ofthe-world.com/capitals-of-africa.html)

- **Countries of the World – List of Countries in Africa**
  - **Description:** Comprehensive list of all sovereign nations on the African continent.
  - **URL:** [https://www.countries-ofthe-world.com/countries-of-africa.html](https://www.countries-ofthe-world.com/countries-of-africa.html)

- **World Atlas – Regions of Africa**
  - **Description:** Reference article describing the five sub-regional classifications of the African continent according to the UN geoscheme.
  - **URL:** [https://www.worldatlas.com/geography/regions-of-africa.html](https://www.worldatlas.com/geography/regions-of-africa.html)

- **ISO Online Browsing Platform (OBP) – Country Codes (iso_alpha3)**
  - **Description:** Official ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code registry used to assign standardized three-letter codes to each African country.
  - **URL:** [https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#search](https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#search)
